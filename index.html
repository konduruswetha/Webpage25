Natsai Onai

Natsai Onai (voiced by Jessica Hayles) is one of the Companions you'll encounter in Hogwarts Legacy. Also known as Natty to her friends, she is a student in the Gryffindor House and can be befriended during your studies to unlock exclusive side quests and opportunities.
